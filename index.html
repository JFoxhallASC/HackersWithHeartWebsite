Why are we here?

The mission...

Technology is the world’s single greatest resource. Too often, however, only those who have the money or the convenience to be around technology get to experience the joy of using and cultivating it. We are going to change that. Our mission is democratize the access to technology to those who don’t typically have exposure to it. Everyone, no matter their circumstance, should be able to climb aboard the express train to the future and experience the cutting edge of whats new.

We plan to do this by bringing technologies such as Myros, circuit boards, drones, go-pros, VR headsets and more to children's hospitals, orphanages, old folks homes, to veterans, and homeless shelters. The goal is take their mind off of the daily burdens of life with technology. Our tech-loving staff would do the leg work. We do the work of visiting these places and displaying our inventory and connecting with the people one on one.

Being a resource for further growth.

Anyone can be a Hacker With Heart. We believe that as long as one has the eagerness to learn and the fascination to grow, they can master anything. In the beginning, our Hackers will simply demo our technology and demonstrate the power of learning how to code. When someone demonstrates an interest in furthering their own learning, we then become a medium through which that becomes possible. We then will work closely with those we visit to provide the online resources and guidance necessary to teach themselves how to code.
